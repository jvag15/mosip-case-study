What is MOSIP?

The Modular Open Source Identity Platform (MOSIP) helps governments deliver secure, inclusive national ID systems. It’s open-source, privacy-first, and supports over 90 million identities worldwide.

With a foundational national ID system in place, governments can build effective civil registries and service delivery systems—powering everything from financial inclusion to healthcare access, disaster relief, and digital governance.

Moodboards, Branding & UX Process

Our visual branding journey began with identifying what MOSIP stands for—empowerment, resilience, and digital equity. We avoided visuals that implied dependency, focusing instead on strength, identity, and inclusion.

Research & Discovery

Through stakeholder interviews and competitor benchmarking, we understood MOSIP’s place in the global ID ecosystem. Insights shaped a human-centered foundation for the brand direction.

UX/Visual Strategy

We designed an experience framework that’s clean, modular, and scalable—mirroring the architecture of the platform itself. Typography, color, and layout were systemized into a design system for global reuse.

Collaboration Model

We worked hand-in-hand with MOSIP’s internal team across time zones, conducting weekly design sprints and async feedback loops that helped scale the system efficiently and transparently.
